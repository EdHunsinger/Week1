The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies



Recipe by: My Grandma

Prep Time:

45 Min

INGREDIENTS
  1. 1 1/2 cup (3 sticks) softened butter
  2. 1 cup brown sugar
  3. 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. 1 Tbl vanilla instant pudding powder
  5. 2 Tbl milk, 2 Tbl vanilla extract
  6. 2 eggs
  7. 4 cups all purpose flour
  8. 2 tsp baking soda
  9. 1/2 tsp salt
  10. 4 cups chocolate chips
  11. 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans(optional)
How to make Mama’s Recipe: The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Beat butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
  3. Stir in pudding mix milk and vanilla extract. Beat in eggs. Add dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.
  5. Place 1 1/2 inch balls of dough 2 inches apart on an un greased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
